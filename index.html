About Seet

Mission

Seet envision health, Education, and economic self-sustainability for youth,women and the community at large

Vission

To promote, protect,encourage,empower,educate, and support poor people to acieve their life goals

Our approach

Our focus is on local communities. Building their knowledge,skills, providing tools and working with them in finding solutions to health, education and social issues are our key areas towards improving their life. In addition, we use participatory method to design, implement, monitor and evaluate our programs

What we do

1

Widow and Orphans

We work with local and international medical, development, social welfare volunteers and groups to bring quality health care and social welfare to widow and orphans

2

Medical Outreach

SEET work with local and international volunteers to bring quality health care to more people especially the poor and in hard to reach areas.

3

HIV/AIDs & TB

SEET contributes to control HIV by expanding health services to vulnerable populations, with a special focus on children, people with disabilities and people living with HIV.

4

Covid-19

In 2020, SEET worked with Buguruni and Vingunguti communities to address the Coronavirus pandemic. Through its volunteer network and partners, we provided health education about masks, distancing and hand washing.

5

Child Health

With funding from Laerdal Foundation, SEET, and the Aga Khan University are implementing a research project to evaluate a proof of need for the disposable clean birth pack with Misoprostol, in Rufiji District Council Pwani region.

6

Malaria

From September 2021, SEET in partnership with Health Tanzania Foundation with funding from Kyeema Foundation implement a malaria project in three isolated villages of Rufiji District Council.
